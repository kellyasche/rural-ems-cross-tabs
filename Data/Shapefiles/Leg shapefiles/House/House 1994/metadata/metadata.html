Section 1 Overview
Originator Minnesota Legislative Coordinating Commission - Geographic Information Services Office (LCC- GIS)
Title House District Boundaries, Minnesota, 1994
Abstract This is a polygon data set of the 1994 Minnesota House Legislative District Boundaries. It displays district numbers. Includes house districts associated attributes. It was derived from U.S. Census Bureau 1990 TIGER/Line files.
Purpose This data was created to display historical 1994 Minnesouta House Legislative District boundaries and to allow geographical review/analysis adhereing to the 1994 MN House Legislative District boundaries.
Time Period of Content Date 1994
Currentness Reference This file is outdated as of 2000 and 2010 Census Bureau redistricting efforts. Boundary updates are completed every decennial census.

Section 2 Data Quality
Attribute Accuracy Attributes represent house district information.
Logical Consistency None
Completeness Complete Statewide
Horizontal Positional Accuracy No assessment completed.
Vertical Positional Accuracy No assessment completed.
Lineage This database of Historical House Legislative District Boundaries was developed from U.S. Census Bureau 1990 TIGER/Line files. The redistricting boundaries were extracted from the 1990 TIGER/Line files using Maptitude software. The attributes associated with the house legislative district boundaries represent district information. This was completed by dissolving census block boundaries to the established house legislative district boundaries.
